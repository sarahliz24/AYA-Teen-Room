What are teen rooms?

Teen rooms are dedicated lounge rooms solely for AYA cancer patients (age 13-24)

Located on the ward, they give AYAs a space to hangout away from their rooms, in an area tailored to AYA interests & needs

Why do we want your feedback?

We are dedicated to listening to you and taking your needs into account - we apply this to everything we do

- your opinion matters

We read & reply to all feedback given, then do our best to put it into action

Why do I have to login to see/give feedback?

This is a safe space for teen room users to express their views without worrying who might read them

You can be anonymous however...

We ask for you medical ID on sign-up just in case we get worried about a post content, so we can follow up for safety reasons
